About Jengu

It is not easy to find an appropriate therapist who can help you recover from your injury or any other accident. You will either go with the referral of your health practitioner or ask for a recommendation from family or friends. In such a scenario the risk of receiving unsatisfactory services is pretty high. We have accumulated some of the finest doctors, therapists, chiropractors, trainers, physiotherapists, and health providers in one place to give you easy and convenient access to professional health services.

JenGu feels pride in playing the role of a mediator and working as a facilitator bridging the gap between clients and health providers. Our goal is to make health services easily accessible for all and create a community where people interact with different therapists and health providers and making new connections. Covering most of the major health disciplines while offering a diversified range of services is something that makes us stand out from the rest.

Jengu Member Portals

The additional features offered by JenGu are the two member portals, one is for health providers and the other one is for training. The portal for health providers is to facilitate them to communicate with the patients in a better way. They would be able to contact the patient via video conferencing and it won’t cost them anything. Preferred for General Practitioners. Some providers like therapists can also post ads on JenGu.

The training portals are for the fitness instructors, they can easily conduct their virtual classes through their personal web portal. The instructors portal will allow them to display their schedule and availability status. They can also post coupons or other promotional deals to attract more clients to avail their services on JenGu.

How Does Jengu Work?

This is a platform for all health providers where they can commercialize their services and gain maximum clients by attracting the audience. The service partners will set up their profiles with all the necessary details and information. The user then can easily search out for his desired services and book an appointment without any hassle.

As we say Help Us Help You, join hands with us and be our partner to boost the growth of your business and make new connections and improved relationships with your clients. Unlike others we don’t keep an eye on the business you get from our site. Apart from our monthly fees, we don’t charge anything for the portals or any other facilities provided to the health providers through JenGu.
