Chiropractor

The health care professional that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of neuromuscular disorders through manual adjustment or adjusting the spine is called a chiropractor. A musculoskeletal chiropractor focuses on spine care to treat back pain, muscle pain, neck pain, and other joints of the body and work on symptom relief. While traditional wellness chiropractors work on improving the overall physical functionality of the body by subluxations and structural correction.
